// WHAT IS PuTTrY

Your terminal.
Everywhere.

A secure, browser-based SSH terminal that keeps sessions alive across every device and network.

Session Persistence

Your terminal sessions stay alive across all your devices. Switch from your phone to laptop without losing your shell.

persistence

Enterprise Security

Session password protected backend with optional 2FA. Passkeys (Touch ID, Face ID, Windows Hello) or TOTP authentication.

Central credential management—no key sprawl across devices. Phishing-resistant and instantly revocable. Rotate access instantly from anywhere with no client-side changes needed.

secure

Team Collaboration

Share terminal sessions with your team in real-time. Multiple viewers, one writer model keeps control.

collaboration

Works Anywhere

Access your servers from any browser on any device. No SSH client installation required.

universal
